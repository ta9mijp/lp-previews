Our Story

More than a meal — a gathering place

Oja African Cuisine brings the heat, colour, and generosity of West African cooking to the heart of Liverpool City Centre. Every dish — from slow-simmered efo riro to peppery snails — is freshly made, abundantly portioned, and built to satisfy. This is food that doesn't whisper.
