Our Story

Your corner of Poplar, cup by cup.

Nest Grocery, Coffee & Wine Shop sits at the heart of Poplar High Street — part café, part larder, entirely yours. Whether you're pulling up a stool for a morning espresso or browsing bottles for a quiet evening in, this is the kind of place that makes a neighbourhood feel like home. Honest produce, carefully chosen wine, and coffee worth crossing the street for.

01

Neighbourhood First

Every item on our shelves and in your cup is chosen with the people of Poplar in mind — no pretension, just quality you can afford daily.

02

Slow Your Morning

We believe a good coffee isn't something you gulp at a counter — it's a reason to pause, breathe, and belong somewhere.

03

Good Taste, Stocked Simply

From natural wines to artisan groceries, we curate only what we'd genuinely bring home ourselves.
