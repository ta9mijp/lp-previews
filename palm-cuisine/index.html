Our Story

Born from the Heart of the Caribbean

Tucked away in the heart of South London, Palm Cuisine is a beloved neighbourhood treasure serving honest, generous Caribbean cooking that keeps guests coming back time and again. From slow-braised oxtail to fragrant curry goat, every dish is crafted with warmth, tradition, and an unmistakable home-cooked soul.
