Our Story

A Taste of Jamaica
in South London

Nestled in the heart of South London, Chillin 876 brings the warmth and bold flavours of Jamaica straight to your plate. Every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and cooked with the kind of love you'd only find in a Jamaican kitchen. Come for the food, stay for the smiles.

Our Menu

Crafted with Soul,
Served with Pride

Bold Jamaican flavours, made fresh every day. A selection of our finest dishes.

Starters

  • Scotch Bonnet Pepper Prawns £9

    Juicy tiger prawns tossed in a fiery scotch bonnet and garlic butter glaze, served with warm festival bread.

  • Jerk Chicken Wings £8

    Crispy, flame-kissed wings marinated overnight in our signature jerk spice blend, served with a cooling mango dip.

  • Saltfish Fritters £7

    Golden, lightly spiced codfish fritters made to a traditional Jamaican recipe, served with a zesty escovitch sauce.

Mains

  • Slow-Cooked Oxtail £18

    Tender oxtail braised low and slow in a rich, herb-laden gravy with butter beans, served with rice and peas.

  • Brown Stew Chicken £15

    Free-range chicken slow-cooked in a deeply savoury brown stew with thyme, allspice, and sweet peppers, served with plantain and rice.

  • Ackee & Saltfish £14

    Jamaica's beloved national dish — silky ackee folded with flaked saltfish, onions, and scotch bonnet, served with fried dumplings and callaloo.

Desserts

  • Rum & Coconut Bread Pudding £7

    Warm, indulgent bread pudding infused with Jamaican rum and toasted coconut, drizzled with a brown sugar caramel sauce.

  • Grater Cake £4

    A classic Jamaican sweet treat of shredded coconut and sugar, set in a beautiful pink and white layer — nostalgic, simple, and utterly delicious.
