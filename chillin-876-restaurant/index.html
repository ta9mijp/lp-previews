Our Story

The Heart Behind Every Plate

Nestled in the heart of South London, Chillin 876 brings the warmth, colour, and bold flavours of Jamaica straight to your plate. Every dish is crafted with fresh ingredients and cooked with the kind of love you'd only find in a true Jamaican kitchen. With a stunning atmosphere, generous portions, and a team that greets you like family, this is more than a meal — it's a vibe.
