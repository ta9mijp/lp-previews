Our Story

Street spices, pure vegetarian, zero compromise.

Bawarchi brings the chaotic joy of Indian street food to Thornton Heath — every dish rooted in the bold, unapologetic flavours you'd find sizzling on a Mumbai roadside. It's entirely vegetarian, which here means nothing is missing and everything pops. Regulars keep coming back not just for the food, but because the staff make you feel like you were expected.

Our Values

✦

Vegetarian, Not Vanilla

Every dish proves that plants, spice, and heat can carry more flavour than anything else on the plate.

✦

Street Food Honesty

No white tablecloth pretension — just the kind of cooking that earns its reputation one returning customer at a time.

✦

Spice With Purpose

Each blend is built to do something specific: lift, warm, linger, or hit hard — never just to fill a gap.
