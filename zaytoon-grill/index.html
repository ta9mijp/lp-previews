Our Story

Heart, Fire &
Honest Flavour

Nestled on the vibrant Peckham High Street, Zaytoon Grill brings warmth, bold flavours, and generous hospitality to every plate. Whether you're grabbing a quick bite between errands or settling in for a proper meal, our team is here to make you feel right at home. Friendly service, honest prices, and food cooked with real care — that's the Zaytoon way.

What We Serve

Our Menu

A selection of our most-loved dishes, crafted with quality ingredients and grilled to perfection.

Starters

  • Hummus & Warm Flatbread £4

    Silky-smooth chickpea hummus drizzled with extra-virgin olive oil and a pinch of smoked paprika, served with freshly baked flatbread.

  • Grilled Halloumi £5

    Golden, lightly charred halloumi slices finished with a squeeze of lemon and a scattering of fresh herbs.

  • Zaytoon Mezze Plate £6

    A curated selection of stuffed vine leaves, tabbouleh, and house-pickled vegetables — a perfect introduction to the grill.

Mains

  • Mixed Grill Platter £14

    A generous spread of marinated lamb kofta, chicken shish, and seekh kebab served with saffron rice, grilled peppers, and garlic sauce.

  • Lamb Chops £15

    Tender, herb-crusted lamb chops char-grilled to perfection, accompanied by roasted tomatoes and a cooling mint yoghurt.

  • Chicken Shawarma Wrap £9

    Slow-cooked spiced chicken carved fresh, wrapped in soft flatbread with crisp salad, pickles, and our signature garlic and chilli sauce.

Desserts

  • Baklava £4

    Layers of crisp, buttered filo pastry packed with crushed pistachios and walnuts, soaked in fragrant rose-water honey syrup.

  • Grilled Pineapple & Honey £4

    Caramelised pineapple spears kissed by the grill, drizzled with wildflower honey and dusted with cinnamon.
