About Zaytoon Grill

Zaytoon Grill is Peckham's beloved neighbourhood grill, serving bold, flame-kissed dishes rooted in the rich traditions of Middle Eastern and Mediterranean cooking. Whether you're stopping in for a quick bite or settling in for a leisurely meal, our welcoming team is here to make every visit feel like home.
