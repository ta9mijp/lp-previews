Our Story

From Naples to a back street in Clerkenwell.

Demo-Vesuvio began as a single notebook of recipes carried over from Naples — sauces that take six hours, dough that rests overnight, and a wood oven we had shipped over piece by piece. Nothing here is rushed, and nothing is off a delivery van.

We still make 40kg of fresh pasta by hand, every morning, before the doors open.
