Our Story

The Heart of Nigerian Cuisine in South London

Tiwa 'N' Tiwa brings the vibrant heart of Nigerian cuisine to South London with rich, spiced stews and grilled specialities that transport your palate across continents. Our kitchen honours traditional recipes with genuine ingredients and passionate preparation, creating dishes worthy of the journey.

Our Philosophy

Bold, Layered & Uncompromising

We celebrate the bold, layered flavours of Nigeria without compromise, crafting stews and marinades that speak to our culinary heritage. Every dish begins with carefully sourced ingredients that honour traditional Nigerian cooking and deliver genuine taste.

Whether you're discovering Nigerian cuisine for the first time or returning home through food, Tiwa 'N' Tiwa is your table.
