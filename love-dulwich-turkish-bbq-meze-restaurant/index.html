Our Story

Fired With Passion,
Served With Love

Nestled on Lordship Lane in the heart of Peckham, Love Dulwich Turkish BBQ & Meze Restaurant brings the warmth, colour, and bold flavours of Turkish cuisine to South London. From smoky charcoal-kissed kebabs to vibrant cold meze platters, every dish is crafted with passion and the finest ingredients.

Whether a planned celebration or a happy discovery, Love promises a dining experience that keeps you coming back.

Our Menu

A Journey Through
Turkish Flavour

🥗

Starters

  • Sigara Böreği £7

    Crispy hand-rolled pastry cigars filled with creamy white cheese and fresh herbs, served with a tangy chilli dip.

  • Humus Kavurma £9

    Silky house-made hummus topped with tender sautéed lamb and toasted pine nuts, served warm with flatbread.

  • Patlıcan Salatası £8

    Flame-roasted aubergine blended with garlic, lemon, and tahini, drizzled with extra virgin olive oil.

🍢

Mains

  • Adana Kebab £18

    Hand-minced spiced lamb moulded on skewers and charcoal-grilled to perfection, served with bulgur pilaf and grilled peppers.

  • Kuzu Şiş £22

    Tender marinated lamb cubes threaded on skewers and cooked over open flame, accompanied by sumac onions and warm flatbread.

  • Tavuk Kanat £16

    Smoky charcoal-grilled chicken wings marinated overnight in Turkish spices and pomegranate molasses, served with a cooling yoghurt sauce.

🍮

Desserts
