Our Story

Nestled on Lordship Lane in the heart of Peckham, Love Dulwich Turkish BBQ & Meze Restaurant brings the bold, smoky soul of Turkish cuisine to South London. Every dish is crafted with passion — from charcoal-kissed kebabs to vibrant mezze platters bursting with colour and flavour. Whether you plan your visit or stumble upon us by chance, we promise a discovery worth savouring.

🔥

Fire & Craft

Every skewer and cut of meat is grilled over open charcoal flames, honouring centuries-old Turkish BBQ tradition.

🫒

Mezze as a Way of Life

We believe great dining begins with sharing, which is why our mezze selection is generous, vibrant, and endlessly inviting.

🤝

Warmth in Every Detail

From the first welcome to the last bite, our team delivers the kind of genuine hospitality that keeps guests coming back.
