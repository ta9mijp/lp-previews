Our Story

A taste of the coast

Rodin Kebab is Shoreditch's go-to destination for bold, flame-grilled kebabs made with the freshest ingredients and served with genuine warmth. Every wrap, every bite, every sauce is crafted with care by a team that truly loves what they do. Come hungry, leave obsessed.

01

Fresh Every Time

We source quality cuts daily and prepare everything in-house so every kebab tastes like it was made just for you.

02

Flavour First

From our perfectly spiced meats to our top-tier sauces, bold and balanced flavour is at the heart of everything we serve.

03

Genuine Hospitality

Our team, led by Zaki, believes great food tastes even better when it's served with a smile and a personal touch.
