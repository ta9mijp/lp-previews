Our Story

Roots, craft & character

Love My Shawarma is a no-nonsense shawarma spot tucked into Bradford City Centre, where every wrap is packed tight with seasoned meat, fresh toppings, and a chilli sauce that earns its reputation. The menu is small by design — because when you do one thing this well, you don't need to do anything else. Fast, friendly, and fiercely good.
