Our Story

Restaurant & Takeaway · South London

Nestled in the heart of South London, Jerk Island brings the vibrant flavours of the Caribbean to your table with every lovingly prepared dish. Our kitchen is led by a passion for tradition, using time-honoured recipes and the finest ingredients to create food that feels like a warm embrace. Come hungry, leave with a story worth telling.
