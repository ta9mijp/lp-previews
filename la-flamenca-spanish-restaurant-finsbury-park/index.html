Our Story

Spain on a plate, worth every mile.

Tucked inside a vibrant food yard in Seven Sisters, La Flamenca brings the heat of Andalusia to North London — one saffron-laced paella at a time. Every dish is built on bold, unapologetic Spanish flavour, from punchy starters to slow-cooked mains that make regulars cross the city twice over. This is the kind of place that earns devoted fans, not just passing customers.
