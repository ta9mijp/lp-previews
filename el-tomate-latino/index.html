Our Story

Latin heat, loud laughs, one unforgettable ta

El Tomate Latino brings the unapologetic warmth of Latin America to the heart of Elephant and Castle — a neighbourhood bar where birthdays turn into parties and strangers leave as friends. The food is bold, the drinks are generous, and the team genuinely cares about every person who walks through the door. This is the kind of place you stumble upon by chance and return to by choice.
