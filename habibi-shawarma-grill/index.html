Our Story

Roots, craft & character

Habibi is Rusholme's warmest corner — a place where hand-carved shawarma, smoky grilled meats and bold Middle Eastern spices land on your table fresh, generous and full of life. The staff greet you like family, because that's exactly how they think of you. This is Manchester's shawarma done right.
