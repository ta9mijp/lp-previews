Our Philosophy

One season, one chance — ichigo ichie.

Demo-Sakura was imagined around a single idea: that a meal should belong entirely to the moment it's served in. Our fish arrives daily from Billingsgate, our vegetables shift with the UK growing calendar, and our charcoal grill never cools before service ends.

Every plate is built by hand, in small batches, by a kitchen that would rather sell out of something good than serve something average.
