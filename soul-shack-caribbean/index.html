Our Story

More than a meal — a gathering place

Soul Shack brings the real flavours of the Caribbean to the heart of Manchester — slow-cooked, spice-forward, and unapologetically bold. From fall-off-the-bone curry goat to crispy fried fish served with sweet plantain and fiery scotch bonnet, every plate is built on tradition and turned up loud. This is the kind of food that makes first-timers into regulars.
