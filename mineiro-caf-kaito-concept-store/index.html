Our Story

Brazilian warmth baked into every bite.

Mineiro Café is Willesden's little corner of Brazil — a concept store and café where the smell of freshly baked pão de queijo pulls you in before you've even opened the door. Born from the food culture of Minas Gerais, every pastry, sandwich, and cup of coffee carries the unhurried generosity of a Brazilian kitchen. Whether you're stopping in for five minutes or staying for an hour, you'll leave a little warmer than when you arrived.

✦

Made With Hands

Every pastry is shaped and baked in-house using recipes rooted in the heartland of Minas Gerais, Brazil.

✦

No Bad Moments

Whether it's a funeral lunch or a Friday treat, we believe food should meet you exactly where you are.

✦

A Store, A Café, A Feeling

Kaito Concept Store wraps coffee, culture, and Brazilian craft into one unpretentious Willesden address.
