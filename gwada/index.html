Our Story

A Taste of Ethiopia in the Heart of Dalston

Gwada is a beloved vegan Ethiopian café in the heart of Dalston, celebrating the rich flavors and traditions of East African cooking. With impeccable service, charming ambiance, and exceptional plant-based dishes, we create an unforgettable dining experience that honors both heritage and ethics.

Plant-Based Authenticity

We craft traditional Ethiopian cuisine entirely from plants, proving that vegan cooking can be deeply satisfying and culturally rooted.

Community & Welcome

Every guest is treated like family, with warm service and genuine hospitality that makes Gwada a true neighborhood gem.

Quality & Flavor
