The Story

Grilled over fire, alive with flavour.

Kozan Grill has arrived in Stoke Newington with the kind of cooking that earns its reputation fast — charcoal-kissed meats, soft warm bread, and a welcome that feels genuinely meant. Every plate comes off the grill with exactly the right amount of char, juice, and care. This is the neighbourhood table you didn't know you were missing.

01

The Grill is Sacred

We cook over real heat because fire does things to meat that no oven ever could.

Fresh Every Day

Ingredients are sourced daily so nothing on your plate has waited longer than it should.

03

Come as You Are

Kozan is a place for long dinners, loud laughter, and going back for seconds without a second thought.
