The Experience

Kingsbury's finest spud, loaded with love.

J'Spuds is Kingsbury's beloved jacket potato kitchen — a neighbourhood staple where every potato arrives properly baked, generously loaded, and honestly priced. We believe a great meal doesn't need a complicated menu, just quality ingredients and people who genuinely care about feeding you well. Come for the spuds, stay for the warmth.
