Our Story

Kerala heat, Seven Sisters hunger, zero compromise.

Kerala Biryani Hut landed on Seven Sisters with a mission: bring the coastal spice trails of southern India to north London, one fragrant pot at a time. A young couple with deep roots in Keralan cooking took over the kitchen and made it their own — fresh ingredients, bold technique, and genuine warmth in every order. Whether you're here for the layered depth of a veggie biryani or the crispy punch of Chicken Manchurian, this is food cooked with something to prove.

Our Values

✦

No shortcuts

Every biryani is built layer by layer, the way it's done back home in Kerala — no batch cooking, no compromise on the spice blend.

✦

Fresh or nothing

If the Chicken Manchurian isn't crispy the moment it leaves the kitchen, it doesn't leave the kitchen.

✦

Neighbourhood first

Seven Sisters deserves food this good, and we're here to prove it one order at a time.
