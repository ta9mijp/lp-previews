The Experience

Jamaica's boldest flavours, planted in Salford.

Sweet Jamdown brings the heat, colour, and soul of Jamaican cooking to the heart of Salford — every dish cooked fresh, every day, without compromise. From smoke-kissed jerk chicken to rice and peas built on generations of know-how, this is food that tastes like somewhere it means something. Walk in a stranger, leave feeling like family.
