Our Story

Bà Ba brings the sun-drenched flavours of Southern Vietnam to the vibrant streets of Peckham, where every dish tells a story of family, memory, and tradition. Named with the warmth of a grandmother's kitchen, we craft honest, soulful Vietnamese cooking in a space that feels like coming home.

🌿

Rooted in Tradition

Every recipe honours the bold, fragrant culinary heritage of Southern Vietnam, passed down through generations and cooked with unwavering respect.

🏠

A Welcome Like Family

From the moment you step through our door, our team ensures you feel guided, cared for, and truly at ease — just as a grandmother would.

🥬

Honest Ingredients, Honest Cooking

We source with care and cook with intention, letting the natural depth of Vietnamese herbs, broths, and spices speak for themselves.
