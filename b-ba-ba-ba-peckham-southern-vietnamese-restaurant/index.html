Our Story

A Kitchen That Cooks Like Your Grandmother

Bà Ba brings the sun-drenched flavours of southern Vietnam to the heart of Peckham, where every dish tells a story of heritage, warmth, and care. Named with the tenderness of a family nickname, our kitchen cooks the way grandmothers do — with patience, love, and nothing to prove.

🌿

Rooted in the South

Every recipe draws from the vibrant, herb-laden culinary traditions of southern Vietnam, honouring the boldness and brightness that define the region.

🤝

Honest Hospitality

From a considered wine recommendation to a quiet corner table, we believe true warmth is found in the smallest, most attentive gestures.

🏘️

Community at the Table

Set in the beating heart of Peckham, Bà Ba is a place where neighbours become friends and every meal feels like a homecoming.
