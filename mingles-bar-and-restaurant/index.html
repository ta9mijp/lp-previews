Our Story

Bold Flavours.
Real Hospitality.

Mingles Bar and Restaurant brings the vibrant tastes of West Africa to Peckham. From traditional Sierra Leonean classics to expertly crafted cocktails, we celebrate bold flavours and genuine hospitality in every dish and drink.
