Our Story

Where West Africa
Meets London

Mingles Bar and Restaurant brings the vibrant tastes of Sierra Leone and West Africa to London. Our knowledgeable team and expertly crafted dishes celebrate traditional recipes with genuine passion, creating an unforgettable dining experience.

🌍

Authentic Heritage

We honour traditional West African cooking methods and time-honoured family recipes passed down through generations.

🍹

Craft & Discovery

Warm Hospitality
