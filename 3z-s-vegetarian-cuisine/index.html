Our Story

Homemade vegetarian warmth in the heart of Rotherhithe.

3Z's is the kind of place Rotherhithe quietly keeps to itself — a one-man kitchen where the owner pulls up a chair, learns your name, and sends out food that tastes like it was made for you specifically. Every dish is homemade, every visit unhurried. Come as a stranger, leave as a regular.

01

Cooked from scratch, every time

Nothing here comes from a packet — every dish is made by hand, in-house, with ingredients chosen for flavour first.

02

The table is always bigger than you think

Whether you're two or twelve, there's always room — even if it means fetching a bench from the back.

03

Vegetarian food that doesn't explain itself

No asterisks, no substitutions — just genuinely satisfying cooking that happens to contain no meat.
