Our Story

Ethiopian warmth rooted deep in Woolwich.

Tsion brings the communal soul of Ethiopian dining to the heart of Woolwich — walls dressed in cultural photography, tables built for sharing, flavours that have travelled far but feel entirely at home. Whether you arrive for a quiet lunch or a long, lingering dinner, the kitchen treats every plate as a gesture of hospitality. Vegetarians and meat-eaters pull up to the same injera-lined spread and leave equally satisfied.

✦

Communal by Nature

Ethiopian food was never meant to be eaten alone — every dish arrives as an invitation to reach across the table.

✦

Spice With Intent

Our berbere and mitmita are blended with purpose, layering warmth and depth rather than chasing heat for its own sake.

✦

Culture on Every Wall

The photography, the colours, the music — Tsion is a living portrait of Ethiopian heritage planted proudly in south-east London.
