Our Story

Lozells wakes up for breakfast like this.

Tucked into the heart of Lozells, Lawa's is the kind of café that earns its reputation one plate at a time — fresh ingredients, generous portions, and staff who actually mean it when they smile. Guests travel across Birmingham for the breakfast alone, and once you've tried it, you'll understand why. Fast, friendly, and unapologetically good.

✦

Fresh, Every Time

Every dish leaves the kitchen clean, bright, and made with ingredients we'd serve our own family.

✦

Friendly by Nature

We move fast so you're never waiting, but never so fast that you feel rushed out the door.

✦

Neighbourhood First

Lozells is home — and this café exists to feed it well, morning after morning.
