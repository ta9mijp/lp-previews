Our Story

Bold regional China, rooted in Poplar.

Chinese Gourmet brings the fire and finesse of regional Chinese cooking — from Sichuan-laced broths to hand-pulled Xi'an noodles — to the heart of east London. This is not a one-note takeaway; it is a kitchen that respects the geography of a vast cuisine, giving each dish its own distinct voice. Whether you arrive hungry for silky dumplings or a bowl of chilli-drenched biang biang noodles, you leave knowing you ate something real.

Our Values

✦

Region Over Generic

Every dish traces its lineage to a specific province — Shaanxi, Sichuan, Cantonese — because geography is the soul of Chinese cooking.

✦

Heat With Purpose

Spice here is a flavour decision, not a shortcut — layered, balanced, and built to complement rather than overwhelm.

✦

A Table for Everyone

From bone-in ginger chicken to tofu biang biang noodles, the menu is written to feed the carnivore and the vegetarian with equal generosity.
