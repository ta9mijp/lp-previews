About Elliott Flava

Elliott Flava brings the vibrant, soulful flavours of the Caribbean to the heart of South London, where every dish is crafted with genuine care and a whole lot of love. From slow-cooked brown stew chicken to smoky jerk lamb, our kitchen serves food that tastes like home — whether you've been here before or not.
