Who We Are

Warmth, Colour & Soul on Every Plate

Asafo Ghanaian Restaurant brings the warmth, colour, and soul of West African cooking to the heart of South London. From our legendary seasoned chicken wings to dishes that taste like home no matter where you're from, every plate is crafted with love and tradition. Whether you're a longtime fan of Ghanaian cuisine or discovering it for the first time, you'll leave feeling like family.

Our Philosophy

Roots, Recipes & Real Flavour

  • 🌿 Roots & Recipes
    Every dish we serve is rooted in the rich culinary traditions of Ghana, prepared with authentic spices and techniques passed down through generations.
  • 🏡 A Taste of Home
    We believe great food transcends borders — creating a space where Ghanaians abroad and curious newcomers alike find warmth, comfort, and belonging.
  • ✨ Simple Done Brilliantly
    We celebrate the beauty of honest, unfussy cooking — bold flavours, quality ingredients, and the kind of food that keeps you coming back.
