Our Story

Asafo Ghanaian Restaurant brings the warmth, colour and bold flavours of West Africa to the heart of South London. From perfectly seasoned chicken wings to fragrant rice dishes, every plate is crafted to feel like a homecoming. Whether you were born in Accra or simply curious about one of the world's great culinary traditions, you are welcome at our table.

🌍

Authenticity Above All

Every dish is rooted in Ghanaian tradition, using spices, techniques and recipes passed down through generations.

🏠

A Feeling of Home

We believe great food should wrap around you like a warm embrace, wherever in the world you find yourself.

✨

Simple Done Perfectly

We honour the beauty of unfussy, well-seasoned cooking — letting honest, quality ingredients speak for themselves.
