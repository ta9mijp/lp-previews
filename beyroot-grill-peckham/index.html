Our Story

The Heart of Lebanese
Grilling in South London

Beyroot Grill Peckham brings the bold, smoky flavours of Lebanese grilling to the heart of South London, where every dish is cooked fresh to order with the finest ingredients.

From tender lamb chunks to perfectly spiced kebabs, each platter is a generous celebration of Middle Eastern culinary tradition. Whether you're a regular or a first-time guest, our warm, welcoming team makes every visit feel like a feast among friends.
