Our Story

Beyroot Grill Peckham brings the bold, smoky traditions of Lebanese grilling to the heart of South London. Every dish is cooked fresh to order, using hand-selected ingredients that honour the flavours of the Levant. Come for the food, stay for the warmth — this is hospitality the way it was meant to be.

🔥

Fresh, Always

Every order is cooked from scratch the moment you place it, because great food simply cannot be rushed or reheated.

🫙

Flavour Without Compromise

From perfectly spiced kebabs to fall-apart lamb, every ingredient is chosen to deliver the most authentic Levantine experience possible.

🤝

A Place to Belong

We believe a great meal is nothing without great atmosphere, and we pour as much care into our welcome as we do into every dish.
