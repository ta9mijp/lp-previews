Our Story

Food that brings people together

Little Deli God Bless is your neighbourhood gem serving authentic Jamaican cuisine with heart and soul. Every dish is crafted with care, and our team—led by the legendary Aimar—treats you like family from the moment you walk through the door. This is more than a restaurant; it's a taste of Jamaica right here in Peckham.
