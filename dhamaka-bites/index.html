The Story

Bold Indian flavours bursting with every single bite

Nestled in the heart of Tooting, Dhamaka Bites brings the vibrant spirit of Indian street food and Gujarati home cooking straight to your table. From crispy chaats to hearty mains, every dish is crafted with authentic recipes and generous portions that won't break the bank. Whether you're dining in or taking away, we promise a flavour-packed experience that keeps you coming back.

01

Authenticity First

Every recipe honours the bold, time-honoured traditions of Gujarati and Indian street food cooking.

02

Flavour for Everyone

We believe extraordinary food should be accessible, affordable, and shared with the people you love.

03

Quality in Every Order

Whether you dine in or order takeaway, we hold every plate to the same high standard of freshness and taste.
