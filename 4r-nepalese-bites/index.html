Our Story

Momos, jhol, and mountains in every bite.

4R Nepalese Bites brings the soul of Kathmandu's street corners to Plaistow — where hand-folded momos arrive steaming and the jhol broth runs deep with spice and memory. Regulars come back three, four, five times, and the kitchen never lets them down. This is Nepalese home cooking served with the kind of quiet pride that no recipe book can teach.

Our Values

✦

Fold by Fold

Every momo is shaped by hand, the way it has always been done — no shortcuts, no shortcuts, no shortcuts.

✦

The Jhol Is the Soul

Our signature broth is ladled generously and refilled freely, because a great momo deserves an even greater dip.

✦

Consistency Is the Compliment

When guests return a third time expecting the same bold flavours, and find them — that is our highest achievement.
