Our Story

Where suya smoke meets celebration table.

Palacess Spicy Suya Garden brings the bold, fire-kissed flavours of West African street grills and home kitchens to the heart of East London. From wedding feasts to weeknight cravings, every order is built with the kind of care that makes guests lean across the table and ask who cooked this. Suya that bites back, jollof that lingers — this is food that earns its reputation plate by plate.

01

The Suya Standard

Every skewer is marinated in our house spice blend and grilled to order — never rushed, never reheated.

02

Jollof Without Compromise

Our jollof rice is cooked low and slow over open heat, chasing that coveted smoky bottom layer every single time.

03

Made for Occasions

Whether it's a wedding reception or a quiet Tuesday, we treat every order as the centrepiece of someone's table.
