About SoulFood Peckham

SoulFood Peckham brings the warmth of Caribbean home cooking to the heart of SE15, serving generous, honest plates that feed the body and lift the spirit. Every dish is crafted with care, from fall-off-the-bone jerk chicken to slow-braised oxtail — flavours that feel like a hug.

🍲

Cooked with Soul

Every recipe carries the deep, slow-cooked traditions of Caribbean kitchens passed down through generations.

🤲

Honest Portions

We believe great food should fill you up without emptying your pockets — quality and generosity go hand in hand here.

🏘

Community First

Born and rooted in Peckham, we cook for our neighbours with the same love we'd cook for family.
