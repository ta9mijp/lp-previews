Our Story

Rooted in the South

Bà Ba — Vietnamese for "grandmother" — is a love letter to the cooking of Southern Vietnam. Bold lemongrass, fragrant herbs, the slow heat of chilli, and the alchemy of a good broth. Every dish on our menu carries a memory from another table, another kitchen, another generation.

We opened on Peckham Rye because we believed this neighbourhood deserved something thoughtful. Not a facsimile, but the real thing — made with care, served with pride.
