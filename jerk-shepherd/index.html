Our Story

Fire, Flavour & Family

Jerk Shepherd was born from a love of authentic Caribbean cooking passed down through generations. Nestled in the heart of Peckham on Bellenden Road, we've been bringing the warmth of the islands to South London — one plate at a time. Every dish is marinated overnight, grilled fresh, and served with the same love you'd find at a family table back in Jamaica.
