The Story

Bold flavours, honest cooking, every single time

Nestled in the heart of Bethnal Green, Amee brings the soul of Caribbean home cooking to East London with every plate we serve. From slow-cooked curry goat to smoky, perfectly seasoned jerk chicken, our food is made with love, care, and recipes that have stood the test of time. Come as a stranger, leave as family — that's the Amee way.

01

Made From Scratch

Every sauce, marinade, and side dish is prepared fresh in-house using time-honoured techniques and quality ingredients.

02

Generous By Nature

We believe great food means leaving the table satisfied, which is why every portion is crafted to fill both your plate and your soul.

03

Community First

Rooted in Bethnal Green, we're proud to serve our neighbourhood with warmth, friendliness, and food that feels like home.
