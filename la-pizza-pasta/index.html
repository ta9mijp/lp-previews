Our Story

Plaistow's corner of honest Italian comfort.

La Pizza & Pasta is the kind of place that stops you in your tracks before you even walk through the door — the smell alone has been known to end journeys early. Rooted in the heart of Plaistow, we cook straightforward Italian food that earns its keep: proper dough, real sauces, no shortcuts. Every plate is made to be finished, not photographed.

I.

Smell First

If the aroma doesn't pull you in from the street, we haven't done our job.

II.

No Fuss, No Fillers

Every ingredient on the plate is there because it earns its place — nothing decorative, everything delicious.

III.

Your Local, Forever

We cook the kind of food that turns first-timers into regulars by the time the bill arrives.
