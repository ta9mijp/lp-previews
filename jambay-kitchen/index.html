Our Story

A Taste of the Caribbean
in the Heart of Dalston

Jambay Kitchen brings the vibrant spirit of Caribbean cuisine to the heart of Dalston. From our cozy, greenery-filled space to every finger-licking bite, we craft an experience that's warm, welcoming, and unforgettably delicious.

Why We're Different

More Than a Meal —
A Caribbean Journey

Step into Jambay Kitchen and feel the island breeze. Our chefs bring decades of heritage to every plate — from smoky jerk marinades to slow-cooked stews bursting with depth and colour. We believe the best dining happens when food tells a story.
