The Story

Where every plate carries a story home.

Momtaz sits quietly in the heart of Neasden, serving the kind of food that reminds you why a shared table matters. From slow-cooked curries to chargrilled steaks, the kitchen draws on bold South Asian flavours without apology or compromise. Come for lunch, linger for the warmth.

01

Portion of Soul

Every dish is built around generosity of flavour, not just volume — though we promise the curry keeps up with the rice.

02

Neighbourhood First

Neasden is not a destination people stumble into, which means every guest who walks through our door chose to be here.

03

No Half Measures

From the soup that opens your meal to the steak that anchors it, we cook each component as if it were the only thing on the menu.
